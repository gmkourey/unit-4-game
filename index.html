The Ultimate Battle Instructions
  1. Pick your player. Your options are Tinky-Winky, Dipsy, Laa-Laa, and Po. Your character will move into the "Chosen Player" area.
  2. The three characters you didn't pick will become the "defenders", choose your first defender. Keep in mind, you will need to fight each defender until you lose all of your health or they lose theirs!
  3. Click the attack button to start fighting your defender. Every time you attack, your attack power will increase 2x!
  4. If the current defender looses all of their health, they will be removed and you will need to pick a new defender. Repeat the same attack process as before
  5. To win, you must defeat all of your defenders without losing all of your health. You lose if you lose all of your health before you are able to defeat all three of your defenders. Good luck!
